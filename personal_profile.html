I am a proud graduate of Sto. Domingo National High School (SDNHS), a public school located in Sto. Domingo, Albay. My specialization was visual arts, which covers drawing, painting, and designing.

I applied for the SPA and passed the audition. I was assigned to the visual arts strand, where I met other talented and creative students. We had classes in art history, theory, and criticism, as well as practical workshops in various media and techniques. We also had opportunities to showcase our works in exhibits, contests, and festivals.

The SPA was not easy. It required a lot of dedication, discipline, and hard work. We had to balance our academic subjects with our art classes. We had to meet deadlines, follow instructions, and accept feedback. We had to deal with pressure, competition, and criticism. But we also had a lot of fun, learning, and growth. We made friends, mentors, and connections. We discovered our strengths, weaknesses, and potentials. We developed our skills, styles, and identities.

The SPA was one of the best experiences of my life. It shaped me as an artist and as a person. It prepared me for the challenges and opportunities of the real world. It gave me a sense of purpose and direction. It inspired me to pursue my dreams and to share my gifts with others. I am grateful to my school, my teachers, and my classmates for being part of my journey. I am proud to be a SPA graduate and a visual artist. I hope to continue honing my craft and to contribute to the preservation and enrichment of Filipino culture and heritage.
